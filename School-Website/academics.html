Academics at Bhavan's

Curriculum and Syllabus

Bhavan's is affiliated to CSBE. We strictly follow the rules and regulations specified by the Board. Having all round development in view the syllabus is structured magnificently by experts and teaching staff under the roof. Active participation in Sports, practical experiences and hands-on the new technologies, daily meditation and shloka chanting, personality development sessions and healthy interactive teaching methodologies assisted with smart classes are a few highlights of the curriculum. Starting from Kindergarten to High School students are trained to be leaders for the modern society.

Co-curricular and Extra Curricular Activities

Bhavan's is known for its spiritual practises. Here at Bhavan's students are taught the importance of our indian culture. Teachers explain concepts using stories insipred from the holy books. Students are motivated to learn from the holy books. We organize shloka reciting events, drama and action play events and we have daily morning prayer.
